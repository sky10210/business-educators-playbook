# Teacher Language Library

## Start Discussion
- Make your decision before talking.
- Defend your reasoning, not your preference.
- What evidence supports your choice?

## Push Thinking
- What tradeoff are you accepting?
- What would change your mind?
- Which stakeholder benefits?
- What is the biggest risk?

## Closing
- Revise your answer based on today's discussion.
- Write the strongest argument you heard.
