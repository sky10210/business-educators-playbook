# Investor Pitch Panel

## Classroom Trailer

Founders have six minutes to convince investors that their idea solves a real problem and can become a sustainable business. The investors have limited capital and competing opportunities. A polished logo will not save weak assumptions. Students must prove demand, explain how the business earns money, and answer questions that expose risk.

## Summary

Students pitch a venture to an investor panel that evaluates customer need, business model, financial logic, execution risk, and founder readiness.

## Example Challenge

Student founders propose a subscription service that helps busy families plan affordable meals and reduce food waste.

## Best Uses

- Entrepreneurship
- Business models
- Financial projections
- Persuasive speaking
- Customer validation
- Question-and-answer practice
