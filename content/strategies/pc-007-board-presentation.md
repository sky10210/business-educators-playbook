# Board Presentation

## Classroom Trailer

The board has reviewed the pre-read. They do not need a summary of every page. They need to know what decision is being requested, why it is justified, what risks remain, and how leadership will measure success.

## Summary

Students present a strategic recommendation to a board, justify it with evidence, address financial and stakeholder consequences, and respond to governance-level questions.
