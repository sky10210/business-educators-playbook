# Crisis Management Team

## Classroom Trailer

At 9:05 a.m., the company learns that a product may be unsafe. At 9:12, a customer video begins spreading online. At 9:20, a reporter requests comment. Student teams must decide what to stop, what to say, who to contact, and how to protect people before the next update changes the situation again.

## Summary

Students form a cross-functional response team that stabilizes a business crisis, communicates with stakeholders, and builds a recovery plan as new information arrives.

## Example Challenge

A beverage company receives reports that several bottles may contain an undeclared allergen while social media posts are spreading rapidly.

## Best Uses

- Risk management
- Public relations
- Operations
- Ethics
- Leadership
- Decision-making under pressure
