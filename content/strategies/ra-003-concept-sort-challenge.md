# Concept Sort Challenge

## Classroom Trailer

Every card belongs somewhere, but several could fit more than one category. Students must decide what matters most, explain the organizing rule, and defend the placements that are likely to be challenged.

## Summary

Students sort terms, examples, scenarios, evidence, or decisions into meaningful categories, defend their placements, and revise the structure when competing interpretations appear.
