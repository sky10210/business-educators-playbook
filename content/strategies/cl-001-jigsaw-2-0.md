# Jigsaw 2.0

## Classroom Trailer

Each student holds one piece of the complete business problem. No one can finish the final challenge alone, so every learner must understand one section well enough to teach it clearly and accurately.

## Summary

Students become specialists on one part of a larger topic, teach that expertise to a new group, and complete an individual synthesis task that requires connections across all sections.
