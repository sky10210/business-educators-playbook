# Fishbowl Discussion

## Classroom Trailer

A small group discusses in the center while the rest of the class studies how the conversation works. Students listen for evidence, questions, assumptions, and decision quality, not just who speaks the most.

## Summary

A small inner circle discusses a business issue while an outer circle observes specific reasoning and communication moves, then roles rotate so students apply the feedback in a stronger second discussion.
