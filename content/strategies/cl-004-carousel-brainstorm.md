# Carousel Brainstorm

## Classroom Trailer

Every station begins with a different business problem. As teams rotate, the page fills with ideas, evidence, cautions, and improvements. The challenge is not adding more words. It is making the thinking better.

## Summary

Teams rotate through a sequence of business prompts, build on prior responses, challenge weak ideas, and synthesize the strongest thinking into a final recommendation or action plan.
