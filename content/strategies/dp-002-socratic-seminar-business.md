# Socratic Seminar for Business

## Classroom Trailer

Students enter with annotated evidence and no teacher lecture waiting for them. One student opens with a question about a company's decision. Another challenges the assumption behind it. A third points to a financial exhibit. The conversation builds because students are responsible for moving it forward.

## Summary

Students use a shared text, case, dataset, or business decision to lead a disciplined discussion built on evidence, questioning, listening, and revision.

## Example Prompt

Students discuss whether a profitable company should close an underperforming location that remains important to the local community.
