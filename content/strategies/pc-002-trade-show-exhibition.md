# Trade Show Exhibition

## Classroom Trailer

The classroom becomes an exhibition floor. Visitors move between booths while student teams demonstrate products, answer questions, collect reactions, and adjust their message. Every team must attract attention without sacrificing substance.

## Summary

Student teams design an interactive exhibit and speak repeatedly with visitors as they explain, demonstrate, and defend a business idea.
