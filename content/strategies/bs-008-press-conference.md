# Press Conference Simulation

## Classroom Trailer

The cameras are on, the public wants answers, and every word matters. Company representatives know more than they can responsibly confirm. Student journalists are searching for contradictions, weak evidence, and unanswered stakeholder concerns. The spokesperson must communicate clearly without guessing, evading, or overpromising.

## Summary

Students prepare a public position, deliver a concise opening statement, answer challenging questions, and maintain accuracy under pressure.

## Example Challenge

A popular event was canceled after a safety concern, leaving customers angry about refunds, travel costs, and communication delays.

## Best Uses

- Public relations
- Media literacy
- Crisis communication
- Executive communication
- Questioning
- Stakeholder trust
