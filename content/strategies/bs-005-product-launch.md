# Product Launch Simulation

## Classroom Trailer

The launch date is fixed, the budget is limited, and every department wants something different. Marketing wants attention, finance wants margin, operations wants simplicity, and customers want value. Student launch teams must align every decision before executives approve the plan.

## Summary

Students coordinate customer research, product design, pricing, distribution, promotion, operations, and risk to build and defend a complete launch plan.

## Example Challenge

A snack company is launching a high-protein product for high school and college athletes with a fixed first-quarter budget.

## Best Uses

- Marketing mix
- Cross-functional planning
- Pricing
- Entrepreneurship
- Operations
- Integrated campaigns
