# Press Conference

## Classroom Trailer

A company decision has created public attention. Cameras are on, reporters are ready, and every answer can build or damage trust. Student representatives must stay accurate, responsive, and composed without avoiding the difficult questions.

## Summary

Students represent an organization facing public questions, deliver a clear opening statement, answer reporters with evidence, and manage difficult or unexpected issues professionally.
