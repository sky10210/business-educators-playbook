# Standards Mastery Portfolio

## Classroom Trailer

The gradebook shows scores, but the portfolio shows learning. Students select evidence, explain what each artifact proves, compare early and revised work, and identify the next step for standards that remain incomplete.

## Summary

Students curate evidence of learning by standard, explain growth across drafts and assessments, identify remaining gaps, and defend mastery in a final portfolio conference or reflection.
