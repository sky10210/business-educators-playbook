# TED-Style Business Talk

## Classroom Trailer

The screen is blank. The speaker has one idea, one audience, and only five minutes. Instead of reading slides, the student opens with a customer story, reveals the business problem, and builds toward one memorable insight the audience can use.

## Summary

Students deliver a focused, idea-driven business talk that explains one important insight through evidence, story, and clear audience takeaways.
