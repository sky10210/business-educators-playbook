How to use the playbook

Scan quickly. Plan confidently. Adjust in real time.

The Playbook is a working reference for business educators—not a book you must read from beginning to end.

01

Start with the objective

Decide whether students need to discuss, practice, create, analyze, present, decide, or reflect.

02

Scan the Quick Scan

Check timing, preparation, movement, collaboration, and course fit.

03

Use the teacher language

Prompts and transitions keep the work focused on evidence, reasoning, and decisions.

04

Adapt without losing the thinking

Shorten rounds or scenarios while protecting the central reasoning students must do.
